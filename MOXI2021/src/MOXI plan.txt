Left: 
Oscilla1 - the standard old Oscilla

Right: 
Oscilla2 - tremolo (low frequency ring modulation)
The frequency of this one is the frequency last sung into Oscilla1 divided by 100

Center:
Oscilla3 - spinning 3D Oscilla
The frequency of this one is the difference between the last frequencies sung into oscilla 1 and oscilla2
The rate of spinning is set by the frequency sung into Oscilla2 divided by 8
